Quickstart

The purpose of this project is to identify key data fields within an image taken from the British Library's collection of historic playbills. On the following page you will be presented with an image and asked to draw rectangles around a particular category of data. To draw a rectangle, click the toggle icon towards the left of the viewer and click and drag over the image. Once you have identified all relevant areas click the Save button to move on to the next task.

Viewer Controls

The following controls are provided towards the left of the viewer:

  • Zoom in
  • Zoom out
  • Reset zoom
  • Fullscreen
  • Toggle selection
  • Clone the most recent selection

Selection controls

The following controls are provided over each selection box:

  • Confirm selection
  • Cancel selection

Click on previously confirmed selection to update it.
